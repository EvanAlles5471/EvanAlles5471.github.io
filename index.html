Yachting is closer than you think!

Yacht Club NAVETRU is a place where your sailing spots will become a reality! Your age, gender and physical fitness are not important, your desire to sail is much more important, and everything else - the latest sports yachts, experienced instructors and real sailing regattas - we already have this and are waiting for you!

About


In just 8 days you will be able to master the basics of sailing from scratch and sail on your own 90% of training is practice on yachts. You won't get bored with hour-long lectures. Only practice, only drive School teachers are prize-winners and champions of international sailing competitions The club regularly conducts training and competition regattas, where you can gain experience.
